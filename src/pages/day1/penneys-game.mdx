Here is a game you can play against the house.

We flip a fair coin over and over, writing `H` for heads and `T` for tails,
until a run of **three flips in a row** matches somebody's bet. You pick a
sequence of three; the house picks one too. We then keep flipping until one of
the two patterns shows up as three *consecutive* flips — whoever's pattern
appears first wins the round.

Pick your bet below, choose how many rounds to watch and how fast to flip, and
press **Watch**. Each round is tallied and colour-coded as it finishes.
